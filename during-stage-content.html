Patients During Treatment

Case studies of patients currently undergoing cancer treatment

Patient Profiles

These patients are currently undergoing various cancer treatments.

Stage III

Anil Verma

Male 48 years Kolkata

Diagnosis

Stage III colorectal cancer with regional lymph node involvement.

Treatment

Neoadjuvant chemoradiation followed by surgery. Currently on adjuvant chemo.

Current Status

Tolerating treatment well with manageable side effects.

Stage II

Meena Desai

Female 52 years Pune

Diagnosis

HER2-positive breast cancer, 3cm tumor, no lymph node spread.

Treatment

Neoadjuvant chemotherapy with trastuzumab, planned for surgery.

Current Status

Good response to chemo, tumor shrinking significantly.

Stage IV

Ravi Khanna

Male 60 years Chennai

Diagnosis

Metastatic prostate cancer with bone metastases.

Treatment

Hormone therapy with novel anti-androgens. Bone-strengthening agents.

Current Status

PSA levels decreasing, pain well-controlled.

Stage III

Kavita Joshi

Female 45 years Ahmedabad

Diagnosis

Ovarian cancer with peritoneal involvement.

Treatment

Debulking surgery followed by IV/IP chemotherapy.

Current Status

Completing third cycle, managing nausea with medications.

Stage II

Sanjay Gupta

Male 35 years Jaipur

Diagnosis

Hodgkin's lymphoma, bulky mediastinal disease.

Treatment

ABVD chemotherapy regimen, planned for 6 cycles.

Current Status

After 2 cycles, PET scan shows excellent response.

Treatment Management Tips

Side Effect Management

Work closely with your care team to manage treatment side effects effectively.

Nutrition During Treatment

Maintain proper nutrition to help your body tolerate treatment better.

Seek counseling or join support groups to help cope with the emotional challenges.
